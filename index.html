Overview¶

Ochonetes¶

Ochonotes is a little add-on you can drop onto your Kubernetes cluster to quickly get started with Ochopod. It comes with a little web-shell and provides lean remoting as well. We embed a bunch of tools that allow you to list, query, deploy and shutdown your Ochopod containers.

It is 100% made of Python with a dash of JQuery for the web-shell !
